Agile Poker

A PubNub AngularJS API Sample App

Agile Poker is a collaborative task estimation tool for agile teams built with AngularJS and powered by PubNub global real-time communication infrastructure.

Here's how it works:
  1. Rally a team together
  2. Sign in & create a room for your estimating
  3. Discuss & Vote to determine the sizing
  4. Reveal votes to see if there's consensus!
